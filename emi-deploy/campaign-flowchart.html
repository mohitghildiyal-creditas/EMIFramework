Phase 4 -- Trigger-Based Extra Campaigning
Trigger A -- Conversion Lag
First Appearance Date Monitor
For each segment, the first appearance date is tracked. If conversion rate is lagging behind expected benchmarks on that date, it triggers additional campaigning to close the gap.

6-Month Average Balance Monitor
Compares each account's current outstanding balance against its 6-month rolling average. If the balance spike exceeds 100%, the account is flagged for immediate campaigning -- a high-intent signal indicating sudden credit usage.

Step 6
Campaign Cycle Complete
Accounts that convert at any stage are tracked as bookings. Remaining non-converters exit the active campaign pool until the next inventory refresh.
Strategy Summary
Tier 1 (PB, LBCL, NV): 5/5 days -- full saturation on segments returning 123-179 Rs/msg
Tier 2 (HP_LC, LP_LC): 3/5 days -- moderate reach on low-churn accounts with fresh potential
Tier 3 (HP_Rest, LP_Rest): 2/5 days -- cost-optimized targeting on regular pool (13-29 Rs/msg)
Drop-off: 3-day uniform re-engagement across all segments
Weekend: Full-base messaging on Sat/Sun -- broad sweep across entire eligible inventory
Triggers: Conversion lag detection per segment + Balance spike >100% over 6-month avg for high-intent targeting
